## Applied in the vault

- Every folder has an `index.md` — that's the zettelkasten idea of *every note reachable from at least one other note*.
- Tag pages in [tags/](../tags/index.md) are the "group by tag" view of the vault, maintained by hand so the vault stays tool-agnostic.
- This MOC is itself an example of *emergent structure* — it wasn't designed before the notes existed; it was written once there were enough notes to need a map.
